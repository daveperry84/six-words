6 Words

Choose words that fit into 6 different categories...but be sure not to choose the same words as your friends.

Game Basics

You will be given 6 categories, i.e. Boys names, movie titles, etc.
Can you choose 6 answers that all begin with a given letter and fit each category?
Oh, I almost forgot, you'll be timed too!!

Customise your game

You can setup the game how you wish.
Click 'Setup Game' and choose the categories you would like to play.
Don't have the time? 'Randomise Game' will select the categories for you.

Play with friends

Send your game link to friends and you can play the same game.
Aim to select answers that your friends will not choose.
Once the time is up, calculate your score.
